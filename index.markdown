I am a mathematician who works on problems that arise in algorithmic <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enumerative_combinatorics" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> enumerative combinatorics. </a> I connect enumerative combinatorics with constructive algebra, effective algebraic geometry, differential algebra and dynamical systems of algebraic correspondences. See my papers or have a look at my <a href="https://github.com/buchacm/slides/blob/main/researchStatementBuchacher.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> research statement. </a>

I received my mathematical education at the University of Vienna and the Johannes Kepler University Linz. I held post-doctoral positions at the <a href="https://www.jku.at/en/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> Johannes Kepler University Linz </a> and the Radon Institute for Computational and Applied Mathematics of the Austrian Academy of Sciences and recently worked as a principal investigator of a research project at the Johannes Kepler University Linz. My work was sponsored by the <a href="https://www.jku.at/en/linz-institute-of-technology/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> Linz Institute of Technology </a> and the State of Upper Austria with the LIT Young Career Award LIT-2022-11-YOU-214.
